About Us

Who We Are

We are CyberXSecure, the Cyber Security Club of Central Institute of Technology, Kokrajhar. The objective of this club is to provide resources to the interested students to help them grow in the field of cyber security.

Our Mission

  • To share knowledge of Cyber Security and Information Security through hands-on sessions.
  • To facilitate interactions with industry experts.
  • To provide a platform to help students learn about cyber security.
